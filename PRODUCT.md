# influencr

## Register
brand (landing pages) / product (app dashboard + auth)

## Users
Brand managers and agencies, 25–45, managing 10–100 influencer partnerships. Paying $99–$500/mo across MightyScout, Grin, Aspire, DocuSign, and HoneyBook. Want one tool that does everything at a fraction of the price.

Also: creators who want a free professional home for media kits, deal tracking, and invoices.

## Brand voice
Sharp. Decisive. Efficient. An operator's tool — not a pretty consumer app.

## Anti-references
- Grin, AspireIQ, MightyScout (blue corporate SaaS)
- Mailchimp (too playful)
- Generic SaaS purple gradient heroes
- Editorial-magazine aesthetic (wrong register)
- Centered-stack hero with icon+card grid
